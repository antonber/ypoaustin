Membership Criteria

Joining YPO Austin is a selective process that typically takes 4‚Äì6 months. Members value peerdom ‚Äî the unique environment of trust, confidentiality, and accountability that defines YPO.

YPO Austin maintains standards that are higher than YPO International, reflecting the caliber and expectations of our chapter.

Age

  • Under 45 years old at the time of joining.

Title

  • President, CEO, Chairperson, Managing Director, Managing Partner, or equivalent top executive role.

Organization Size

  • Option A: 50+ full-time employees, or
  • Option B: 15+ full-time employees with a minimum of $2.5M annual employee compensation.

Revenue or Value Thresholds

  • Sales / Service / Manufacturing: $20M+ annual revenue.
  • Agency: $15M+ annual revenue.
  • Financial Institution: $350M+ assets under management.
  • Enterprise Value: $30M+.

If you meet these criteria and your primary residence and company are in the Austin area, we encourage you to apply to YPO Austin as your home chapter.
